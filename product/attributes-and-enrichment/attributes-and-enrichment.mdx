title: "Attributes & Enrichment"
description: "Configure attributes, write AI prompts, run enrichment, and review AI-generated content for your product catalog."
---

This section covers the core of Merchkit's data enrichment workflow. You'll learn how attributes work as both data columns and AI instructions, how to configure them for enrichment, write effective prompts, constrain output with acceptable values, and run enrichment across your catalog.

Start with **Understanding Attributes in Merchkit** to learn the fundamentals, then work through the articles in order. By the end of this section, you'll be able to configure attributes, run AI enrichment, review and edit results, and scale enrichment across large catalogs.
