---
title: "Integrations & Field Mapping"
description: "Connect Merchkit to external platforms, configure import/export field mapping, and manage data syncing."
---

Integrations are the data pipes that connect your external platforms to Merchkit. This section covers everything from connecting your first integration and understanding field mapping, to configuring import/export mappings, working with metafields and variants, and triggering syncs.

Start with **How Integrations Work** for the big picture, then work through the articles in order. By the end of this section, you'll be able to import product data from any supported platform, enrich it in Merchkit, and export your changes back.
