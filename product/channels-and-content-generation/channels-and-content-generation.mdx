This section covers everything about generating marketplace-ready product content in Merchkit. Channels are specialized content generation destinations where Merchkit transforms your core product data into fully formatted, marketplace-specific listings.

Start with **How Channels Work in Merchkit** for an overview of what channels are and how they differ from your Products view, then follow the guides in order to connect channels, generate content, validate, and export to your marketplaces.
