# Adding a Channel

Adding a channel to Merchkit takes just a few clicks. When you select a channel, Merchkit automatically loads the channel's template attributes, category views, and default AI prompts — everything is ready to go.

## Step 1: Open the Channels Section

In your Merchkit sidebar, locate the **Channels** section. You'll see icons for any channels you've already added.

[SCREENSHOT: Channels section in sidebar]

## Step 2: Click "+ Add Channel"

At the bottom of the Channels section, click the **+ Add Channel** button.

A modal window opens showing all available channels organized by type.

[SCREENSHOT: Add Channel modal with available channels]

## Step 3: Select Your Channel

Browse the available channels and click the one you want to add:

- **Wayfair** — Furniture and home décor marketplace
- **Walmart** — Major retail marketplace
- **Amazon** — E-commerce giant
- **Google Merchant** — Google Shopping feeds
- **Shopify** — Your own storefront
- **Mirakl platforms** — (Mathis, Mine, Rue La La, etc.)
- **Nordstrom Rack** — Luxury marketplace
- **ChatGPT / GEO** — AI assistant channels
- **Houzz** — Home design marketplace

[SCREENSHOT: Channel selection list]

Once you select a channel, Merchkit immediately loads the channel's attribute templates and default AI prompts. The channel appears in your sidebar — click it to open its dedicated grid view.

[SCREENSHOT: Channel added to sidebar]

## What Happens Next

Once your channel is added:

- **The channel grid view opens** — You see all channel-specific attributes ready to be filled
- **Default prompts are loaded** — Merchkit pre-configures AI prompts for common attributes
- **You're ready to generate** — Select SKUs and generate channel content

If this is your first time adding a channel, start with [Understanding the Channel Grid View](/product/channels-and-content-generation/channel-grid-view) to see how to navigate and use your new channel.

## Next Steps

Now that your channel is added, explore [Understanding the Channel Grid View](/product/channels-and-content-generation/channel-grid-view) to learn how to navigate and work with channel-specific attributes.
